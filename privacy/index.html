Legal · Privacy

Privacy, explained without the fog.

This policy describes the information handled through the public Barrier Intelligence website and direct enquiries.

This notice covers the public website. Any plant data, safety studies or engineering files used in an Assure3D pilot are handled under a separate written pilot or customer agreement.

1. Scope and who we are

Barrier Intelligence ("Barrier Intelligence", "we", "us" or "our") operates this website to explain our products, evidence model and pilot programme. For questions about this policy or a privacy request, email hello@barrierintelligence.com with the subject "Privacy request".

This policy does not apply to third-party websites, services or customer systems that may be linked from the site.

2. Information we handle

Information you provide

If you contact us by email, we may receive your name, work contact details, organisation, role, message and any other information you choose to include. Please do not send confidential plant information, safety studies or personal data through a general website enquiry.

Website and device information

Our hosting provider may process standard technical logs such as IP address, browser and device type, requested pages, timestamps, referring page and security events to deliver and protect the site.

Current pilot form behaviour

The pilot form on this version of the website is a front-end demonstration. Submitting it displays a confirmation in your browser but does not transmit the entered fields to Barrier Intelligence. To make an enquiry, use the email address above.

3. How and why we use information

Where we receive personal information, we use it only as reasonably necessary to:

  • respond to enquiries and discuss a potential pilot or commercial relationship;
  • take steps requested by you before entering into a contract;
  • operate, maintain, diagnose and secure the website;
  • protect our rights, users and systems, and prevent misuse; and
  • meet legal, regulatory, accounting or compliance obligations.

Depending on the circumstances and applicable law, we rely on steps taken at your request before a contract, our legitimate interests in operating and securing our business, consent where specifically requested, or compliance with legal obligations.

4. Cookies and local storage

We do not currently use advertising or behavioural analytics cookies on this website. The site stores your light or dark theme preference in your browser's local storage so the choice persists between pages. You can remove that preference through your browser settings.

5. Sharing and international processing

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose limited information to hosting, infrastructure and professional-service providers that support the website or our business; to advisers under duties of confidentiality; or to authorities when required by law or necessary to protect legal rights.

Those providers may process information in countries other than your own. Where applicable law requires it, we use an approved transfer mechanism or another recognised safeguard.

6. Retention and security

We retain enquiry information only for as long as needed to respond, evaluate a relationship, maintain appropriate business records or meet legal obligations. Hosting and security logs are retained according to the relevant provider configuration and operational need.

We use reasonable organisational and technical measures intended to protect information. No internet transmission or storage system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction or portability of personal information, to object to certain processing, or to withdraw consent. You may also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

To exercise a right, email us with enough detail to understand the request. We may need to verify your identity and may retain a record of the request where required by law.

Children

This business website is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly seek personal information from children.

8. Changes and contact

We may update this policy as the website, product or applicable requirements change. The revised version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to hello@barrierintelligence.com.
