Verification method

Every verdict has a chain of custody.

Barrier Intelligence connects each conclusion back to the source document, spatial assumption, physical scenario and detector response that produced it. The result can be checked, challenged and signed.

The golden path

Six controlled steps. No unexplained jump from input to claim.

The system accelerates the engineering work while keeping the judgment visible and reviewable.

Establish the plant basis

Define the unit, assessment boundary, target coverage and the Fire & Gas credit already being claimed.

Audit the inputs

Parse the HAZOP, LOPA, philosophy, register, mapping report and drawings with page-level provenance.

Reconstruct the spatial case

Align leak nodes, detector coordinates, obstructions and voting logic to the 3D plant model.

Sweep credible physics

Test releases across wind direction, speed, stability and geometry-driven dispersion behaviour.

Apply the credit wall

Allow only physical performance to earn credit; evidence confidence can demote a verdict but never promote it.

Issue the assurance package

Export the register, overlays, gaps, remediation priorities, method and limitations for engineer sign-off.

Three kinds of truth

The method resolves the basis, the physics and the credit.

Each stage answers a different question before the barrier verdict can be trusted.

01 / Basis

Reconstruct the claim

Turn scenarios, assumptions and detector data into a structured basis without losing the document, page or revision behind each fact.

02 / Physics

Sweep credible reality

Test release behaviour through the plant geometry, detector field and full relevant wind space instead of relying on nominal reach.

03 / Credit

Issue the credit verdict

Let demonstrated detector response determine the state, then retain limitations, evidence quality and engineering authority around it.
